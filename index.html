PONK

The year is 2023. You're standing in front of a table illuminuted with graphics from above by a projector. You have just become a fashionable Ponk Ass and you feel good. In Ponk you play a game over Pong, but control your player paddle via an arduino powered controller attached to the top of you head. Leaning your head to each side sends your paddle in that direction.
